HL7 to FHIR Data Integration for Clinical Systems

Role: Jr. Data Analyst | Organization: Solstice Solutions, Inc. | Period: Oct 2025 to Present

Problem

Clinical systems were exchanging messages with schema and interoperability gaps, increasing deployment friction and rework.

Approach

  • Mapped inbound HL7 structures into target FHIR resources for consistent downstream consumption.
  • Validated transformed data to prevent schema mismatches and improve quality checks before deployment.
  • Standardized mapping patterns and handoff documentation for repeatable delivery.

Outcome

Improved deployment readiness for healthcare integrations by reducing integration errors and improving consistency of transformed data.

Interoperability

improved for cross-system exchange

Validation-first

workflow for schema safety

Reusable

mapping and QA playbooks

Tools and Methods

HL7, FHIR, dataset validation, transformation checks, mapping documentation.
