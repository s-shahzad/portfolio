Enterprise Risk and Threat Analysis (Internship)

Role: IT Risk Analyst Intern | Organization: iQ4 | Period: May 2024 to Aug 2024

Problem

Security findings were scattered across teams and not consistently tied to business impact, making remediation priorities unclear.

Approach

  • Reviewed controls and incident patterns from assessments and operations reports.
  • Mapped observations to NIST-aligned risk categories for consistent triage.
  • Converted technical findings into business-facing risk statements with remediation priority.

Outcome

Produced a prioritized risk view that improved decision speed for remediation planning and cross-team communication.

Tools and Methods
