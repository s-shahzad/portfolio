Enterprise Risk and Threat Analysis (Internship)

Role: IT Risk Analyst Intern | Organization: iQ4 | Period: May 2024 to Aug 2024

Translated fragmented security findings into a clearer, business-prioritized remediation path that was easier for teams to act on.

Scope

NIST-aligned triage

Used a consistent risk framework to organize findings and incident signals.

Outcome

Faster prioritization

Made remediation conversations easier by tying technical issues to business impact.

Value

Clearer handoff

Improved how stakeholders could understand, sequence, and approve follow-up actions.

Problem

Security findings were scattered across teams and not consistently tied to business impact, making remediation priorities unclear.

Approach

  • Reviewed controls and incident patterns from assessments and operations reports.
  • Mapped observations to NIST-aligned risk categories for consistent triage.
  • Converted technical findings into business-facing risk statements with remediation priority.

Outcome

Produced a prioritized risk view that improved decision speed for remediation planning and cross-team communication.

Business Value

The key result was not just a cleaner report. It was a more actionable sequence for remediation, clearer communication with stakeholders, and less time lost translating security language into business priorities.
